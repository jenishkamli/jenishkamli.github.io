📁 Case Studies

How I Turned Challenges Into Growth

Real problems. Real strategy. Real results. Here's how I approach marketplace growth challenges.

01
Problem

Low Organic Visibility on Flipkart

Products were buried on page 4–6 despite competitive pricing. Zero impressions from organic search, leaving ad spend as the only discovery mechanism.

2.8L → 20.2L Impressions
02
Strategy

Keyword-First Listing Restructure

Conducted deep keyword gap analysis, rebuilt titles with high-intent search terms, rewrote bullet points for conversion, and added A+ content to boost algorithm signals and CTR.

A+ Content + SEO Rewrite
03
Result

7× Impression Growth in 5 Months

Organic impressions exploded from 2.8L to 20.2L. Reduced CPC by 34%, improved ROAS, and established top-3 ranking for 12+ high-volume keywords.

ROAS ↑ · CPC ↓ 34%
04
Problem

Amazon Catalog Inconsistency

Over 300 SKUs with inconsistent titles, missing attributes, and poor image quality causing suppression alerts and low buy-box win rate.

300+ SKUs Optimized
05
Strategy

Bulk Catalog Overhaul + Compliance

Built systematic bulk upload templates, enforced brand style guidelines, resolved suppression with Amazon Seller Support, and implemented review management SOPs.

Zero Suppression Alerts
06
Result

Meesho Scale-Up from Zero to Revenue

Set up entire Meesho storefront, onboarded 150+ products, configured ads for new product launch categories. Achieved first 1000 orders within 45 days of launch.

1000 Orders · 45 Days
